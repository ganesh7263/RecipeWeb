Chicken Vindaloo

"Vindaloo is a popular Indian curry dish that calls for a meat to be marinated in a highly flavourly spicy mixture with vinegar, then quickly cooked up when you're ready to eat.

While this dish has a long list of spices, most are quite common in the average spice rack. If you find you're missing one, it's not a deal breaker. You still have a lovely, fragrant curry.
